Overview

This complete hardware automation kit lets you manage your home appliances directly from your smartphone. User Manual & Installation Guide

⚠️ Safety Warning: Turn off your main power supply switch before starting the installation process.

Step 1: Powering the Board

Connect your 5V/12V DC power adapter input to the dedicated power terminal pins on the board layout.

Step 2: Load Connections

Wire your home appliances (like lights or fans) to the Relay output terminals. Connect the live line through the Common (COM) and Normally Open (NO) ports.

Step 3: App Configuration

Turn on your smartphone's Wi-Fi settings. Open your companion application, scan for the hardware hotspot, and type in your local Wi-Fi credentials to link the unit.
